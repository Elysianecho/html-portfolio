The best movies according to Benny

My Top 3 favourite movies

1: Pacific Rim

Pacific Rim is a movie based on the sci-fi world where Gigantic alien creatures called Kaij's start appearing from the pacific and attacking civilizations. However, humanity makes a stand by creating giant advanced robots called Yaegers which are tailored with unique features and designs to fight kaijus. Yaegers are piloted by 2 human pilots who have a strong neurological-mental compatibility.

2: Grown Ups

Grown ups is a 2 sequel movie that focuses on the lives of 5 childhood friends who reconnect and spend time together and have fun with their families while staying at a cabin by the lake.They find growth emotionally and also strengthen their bonds as friends

3: Transformers

The transformers movie is based on a teenager named Sam Whitwicky who buys a car that happens to be an alien robot that can assume a hmanoid figure or disguise itself as a car. It follows their story of action and adventure as other robots land on earth. The robots are broken into two factions; The autobots who are seemingly good while the other faction, the decepticons are a force of evil and rebellion.
